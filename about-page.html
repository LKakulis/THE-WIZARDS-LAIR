THE WIZARD'S LAIR

This experience is THE WIZARD'S LAIR, an interactive website in which you may explore the whimsical wizard's wacky collection. Carved deep in a mountain accessible only by a secret portal where only the bravest proceed, his chamber is a vast obsidian-lined space filled with artifacts and minutiae collected over millenia to brew potions, cast spells, and make breakfast. The air in this wizard's lair hangs thick and still, smelling faintly of ozone, old parchment, and dried rodent entrails. Each object has a unique story for you to uncover with the help of the WIZARD'S fantastical quips. Be amazed at the wonderful nostalgic wizard graphics!!! This website was the beautiful wizardly brainchild of Lukas Kakulis in collaboration with Eddy Ruszczyk
